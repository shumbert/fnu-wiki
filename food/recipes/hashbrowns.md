# Recipe
## stuff
- 3 mid potatoes
- 4 tbsp flour
- 1 large egg
- 1 small onion (finely chopped)
- garlic (grated)
- salt + pepper
- chili powder
- coriander (chopped)

## cook it
- shred potatoes over a bowl water (to prevent potatoes from turning brown)
- when ready drain and squeeze dry
- mix everything in a bowl
- pan -> oil -> put mixture and shap the hashbrowns
